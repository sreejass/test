Advanced Learner Loan

Application form

Remember you can apply online. It’s quick and easy. Go to www.gov.uk/advancedlearnerloan

About this form

Complete this form if:

  • you’re studying an eligible course for a level 3,4,5 or 6 qualification at an approved college or training provider in England
  • you’re aged 19 or over on the first day of your course, if your course started on or after 1st August 2016

Find out more at:

www.gov.uk/advancedlearnerloan

Before completing this form

It’s important you speak to your college or training organisation who will give you a Learning and Funding Information Letter. You’ll need this to complete your form.

What sections should you complete?

Complete all sections. Remember to read, sign and date the declaration on pages 9 and 10.

Please read the accompanying notes

It’s important you read the accompanying notes. The notes are split into three parts:

Part 1 - gives general information about the application process.

Part 2 - will help you to complete each section of the form.

Part 3 - explains steps in the application journey.
